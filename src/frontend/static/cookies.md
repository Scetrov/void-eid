## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to store data that can be recalled by a web server in the domain that placed the cookie.

## 2. No Tracking/Analytics Cookies

We respect your privacy. **We do not use third-party analytics cookies** (such as Google Analytics, Mixpanel, or Facebook Pixel). We do not track your activity across other websites.

## 3. Cookies We Use

We only use "Strictly Necessary" cookies required for the Service to function:

| Name | Type | Purpose | Duration |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `token` / `jwt` | First-Party | Stores your session authentication token after Discord login. | Session |
| `__cf_bm` | Third-Party (Cloudflare) | Used by Cloudflare to distinguish between humans and bots. This is necessary for security. | 30 mins |
| `cf_clearance` | Third-Party (Cloudflare) | Stores proof of passing a challenge (e.g., Captcha) to access the site. | Varies |

## 4. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, if you limit the ability of websites to set cookies, you may be unable to log in or use the linking features of this Service.
